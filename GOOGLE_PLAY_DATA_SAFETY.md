# Google Play Data Safety Configuration

## Data Collection & Usage

### Personal Information
- **Name**: Collected for account creation and personalization
- **Email**: Used for account verification and communication
- **Phone Number**: Required for account verification and security
- **Profile Picture**: Optional, for user identification
- **Date of Birth**: Required for age verification and content filtering

### Location Data
- **Precise Location**: Used for map features and nearby friend discovery
- **Approximate Location**: Used for regional content and recommendations
- **Data Sharing**: Location data is not shared with third parties

### Photos & Videos
- **Camera Access**: Used for taking photos and videos within the app
- **Photo Library**: Used for selecting existing photos to share
- **Content Moderation**: All photos and videos are scanned for inappropriate content
- **Data Sharing**: User-generated content is only shared with intended recipients

### Audio
- **Microphone**: Used for recording voice messages and video audio
- **Music Library**: Used for adding music to videos (with user selection)
- **Data Sharing**: Audio data is not shared with third parties

### Device Information
- **Device ID**: Used for app functionality and analytics
- **App Activity**: Used for personalization and app improvement
- **Crash Reports**: Used for debugging and app stability
- **Performance Data**: Used for optimizing app performance

## Data Security & Privacy

### Encryption
- All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
- Sensitive data is encrypted at rest
- End-to-end encryption for direct messages

### Data Retention
- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Messages: Stored for 30 days after deletion
- Stories: Automatically deleted after 24 hours
- Log data: Retained for 12 months for security analysis

### User Controls
- Users can delete their account and all associated data
- Users can control privacy settings and data sharing
- Users can export their data in machine-readable format
- Users can opt out of data collection for advertising

## Third-Party Services

### Firebase Analytics
- Used for app analytics and performance monitoring
- Data is anonymized and aggregated
- Users can opt out of analytics data collection

### Google Maps
- Used for location-based features
- Google's privacy policy applies to map data
- Location data is not shared with Google beyond map functionality

### AdMob
- Used for displaying advertisements
- Ad personalization can be disabled by users
- AdMob's privacy policy applies to ad-related data

### Push Notifications
- Used for sending app notifications
- Users can disable notifications in device settings
- Notification data is not shared with third parties

## Compliance

### COPPA Compliance
- App is not intended for children under 13
- Age verification is required during registration
- Users under 18 have restricted access to certain features

### GDPR Compliance
- Users have right to access, modify, and delete their data
- Data processing is based on legitimate interest and consent
- Users can withdraw consent at any time

### CCPA Compliance
- Users have right to know what data is collected
- Users have right to delete their data
- Users have right to opt out of data sales (we don't sell data)
